    About Us

    Helping Hand Org is a non governmental foundation with a focus on Education,
    Girl Child Development and Commmunity Projects in Under-developed Societies.


    HH Org is the avenue for an Hand to be all Hands.

    • We target area(s) of acute attention/s through diligent researches.
    • Co-ordinate with our members in funding, logistics and developmental projects.
    • Ensure that project are people centered and community beneficial.
    • Ensure sustainability of projects.

    HH Org reaches out by Helping hundreds of communities in Africa every year in pursiut of her goal.

    Area Of Interest

    Education Support

    Read more about our Classroom for all projects, eduaction material support and Leader of tommorrow project.

    Child Labour Rescue

    We work with local legal advocacy groups and local government authorities to take hundreds of childrens off the child labour every year.

    Girl Child Education

    Our no Gender Education supports have encouraged hundreds of girl to go to school.

    Action Against Child Marriage

    By active and ongoing communities awareness programmes and collaborations with governments authorities, child marriages have reduced with 53% over 21 months.

    Community Support Projects

    We regularly support remote communities with classrooms projects, local health centres renovations and water bore-hole projects.

    Effective Networking

    We work with everyone, governmnets agencies, civil rights organisations, other Foundations. We actively network for effeciency and effectiveness.
